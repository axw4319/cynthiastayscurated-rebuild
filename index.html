My Story

A little about my journey...

I'm Cynthia - a travel advisor, short-term rental designer, and the heart behind Cynthia Stays Curated.

I don't just love beautiful places. I care about how they work!

The spaces people remember, the ones they return to, recommend, and never want to leave are not accidental. They are intentional.

Before this, I built my work around systems, understanding how things flow, where they break down, and how small details shape the overall experience.

I've always been drawn to what most people overlook, the friction points, the missed moments, the details that quietly define how something feels.

Over time, I realized something, the same thinking that creates seamless systems, creates seamless stays and thoughtfully planned travel.

So I brought them together.
