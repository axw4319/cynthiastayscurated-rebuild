Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Cynthia Stays Curated is committed to making cynthiastayscurated.com accessible to as many people as possible, including visitors who rely on assistive technologies. We believe that a welcoming website is part of hospitality, and we work continuously to remove barriers to access.

Our Commitment

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological needs.

Measures Taken

To make our website accessible, we have:

  • Included descriptive alternative text on meaningful images
  • Used semantic HTML to support screen reader navigation
  • Maintained sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Ensured interactive elements are reachable with a keyboard
  • Provided clear page titles, headings, and link labels
  • Tested our forms with common assistive technologies

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some portions of our website may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to improve these areas. If you encounter content that is difficult to use or understand, please let us know — your feedback helps us improve.

Reporting an Issue

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our website, or if you have suggestions for how we can improve, please contact us at:

Email: cynthiastayscurated@gmail.com

We aim to respond to accessibility inquiries within 5 business days and will do our best to provide the information or service you need in an alternative format.

Third-Party Content

Some content on this website is provided by third parties (for example, embedded maps, social feeds, or booking platforms). We do not control the accessibility of third-party content, but we will make reasonable efforts to provide the information in an alternative format upon request.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Cynthia Stays Curated. Please let us know if you encounter any barriers — we take every report seriously and use it to make our site better for everyone.
